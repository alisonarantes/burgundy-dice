Rules Summary

Turn Structure

Roll dice. You can use 1 number die + 1 color die to mark an adjacent valid hex. Or, if you have Silver, use it to mark the remaining pair.

Hex Placement Rules

  • City (Orange): Needs different numbers in the same area.
  • Pasture (Yellow): Needs identical numbers in the same area.
  • Monastery (Purple): Must be 1 or 2.
  • Mine (Gray): Must be 3 or 4.
  • River (Blue): Must be 5 or 6.
  • Castle (Green): Must match a number of any marked neighbor. Grants instant bonus: Workers, Monks, Silver, or Commodities depending on its icon.

Resources & Castles

  • Worker (Orange Icon): +/- 1 to a number die (wraps 1 to 6). Max 1 per turn.
  • Monk (Purple Icon): Change a color die to any color. Max 1 per turn.
  • Silver (Gray Icon): Grants a second action per turn (allows placing the second pair of dice). Max 1 per turn.
  • Commodity (Blue Icon): Automatically sold when a double hourglass (‚è≥‚è≥) is rolled. Gives 1 Silver and 2 VP per commodity sold.

Scoring & Area Bonuses

Completing a colored area immediately awards Victory Points (VP) based on its size, plus a Phase Completion bonus.

When the last hex of an area is filled, you gain a Bonus based on its color:
  • Orange (City): 1 Worker
  • Blue (River): 1 Commodity
  • Purple (Monastery): 1 Monk
  • Yellow (Pasture): Double VP for the Area!
